VR Policy Clarifier

Tier 1 Prompt Template for Vocational Rehabilitation Policy Interpretation

The VR Policy Clarifier is a reusable prompt-template artifact that helps Vocational Rehabilitation counselors interpret gray-area policy questions using only the policy, memo, FAQ, procedure, or manual text they provide. It is designed for decision support only. It does not replace a supervisor, Policy Program Coordinator, official manual, leadership approval, or legal guidance.

Tier 1 Prompt Template Decision Support Only Human-in-the-Loop No Data Stored
!

Use de-identified information only

Do not enter customer names, Social Security numbers, addresses, phone numbers, medical details, employer names tied to a specific customer, or any other identifying information.

This tool uses only the policy text provided by the counselor. It does not retrieve policy, access case records, make final decisions, authorize services, determine eligibility, close cases, communicate with customers, or replace supervisor/leadership review.

Build v1 Scope

Tier 1 prompt-template artifact

  • This is a Tier 1 prompt-template artifact.
  • It does not call an AI API.
  • It does not retrieve policy.
  • It does not store or transmit data.
  • It does not make final VR decisions.
  • It supports counselor reasoning and human review.
Business Problem

Policy interpretation needs a repeatable, source-grounded workflow.

VR counselors often need to interpret gray-area documentation and service questions quickly while staying aligned with policy, confidentiality rules, and supervisory review expectations. This artifact provides a standardized prompt wrapper so the counselor can give an approved AI tool only the relevant de-identified facts and policy text.

Guardrails

What this artifact will and will not do

  • Uses only the policy text pasted into the builder.
  • Frames the AI response as decision support, not final authority.
  • Requires human review before case action or documentation.
  • Does not store, transmit, retrieve, or analyze data outside this page.
  • Flags review when a question is unsupported, high-risk, or exception-based.

No data is stored or transmitted. Everything happens in your browser.

Sample Review

Expected Response Pattern for Sample Scenario

Output Section Expected Content
Direct Answer Likely two Actual Services are needed if both Counseling & Guidance and Person-Centered Practices were actually provided, and a Customer Contact Case Note is also needed.
Policy Basis The provided policy summary treats C&G and PCP as distinct services and requires a Case Note when an Actual Service is entered for either.
Gray Area If the meeting was only scheduling, administrative follow-up, or general case management, it may not meet the definition of either service.
Documentation Steps Document the appropriate Actual Service or Services, then document Customer Contact in a Case Note tied to the service date.
Review Flag No for the basic fact pattern; Maybe if the counselor is unsure whether the interaction qualifies as C&G, PCP, or case management.
Workflow

How to Use This Build

  1. Remove identifying information.
  2. Paste the counselor question.
  3. Add de-identified case context.
  4. Paste the relevant policy text.
  5. Enter the action being considered.
  6. Generate the prompt.
  7. Copy the prompt into the AI tool approved for the course or workplace.
  8. Review the AI response against the actual policy.
  9. Escalate when the response flags review or when uncertainty remains.
